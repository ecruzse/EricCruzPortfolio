Humidity Tracker

Monitoring data such is especially important when it comes to taking care of living things. Taking care of plants I required monitoring of their ambient temperature and humidity to ensure proper growth and maintenance. The date is recorded as well to look at any possible trends that could hurt or help the plants' health.
