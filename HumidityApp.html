Humidity Tracker

Monitoring data is essential for caring for living organisms. For plants, tracking environmental factors such as ambient temperature and humidity is essential to support optimal growth and health. Recording data over time enables the discovery of trends that can have an effect on plant well-being.

Components

  • RaspberryPi3
  • DHT11 Sensor
  • RaspberryPi ZeroW
  • Breadboard

Software

  • Ubuntu Server
  • Python, Flask
  • MySQL/MariaDB
